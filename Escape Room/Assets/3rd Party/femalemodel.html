Description
This model is a free sample from my Female Basemesh models.

A female teen aged body type base mesh. Rigged with a basic IK biped rig in Blender and an FK biped rig for .FBX. No facial rig.

Usable primarily for sculpting and quick posing. This speeds up the creation of teen aged body type characters. Just reshape, sculpt, bake textures and pose.

The model is UV unwrapped.

Topology is higher density on the important parts (head, hands) and mostly even on the rest of the body with minimal face stretching.

Topology allows for good deformations.

Inside of the mouth is not modeled.

Please don't forget to rate&comment if you buy.

Thank you.
